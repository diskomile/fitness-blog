## Introduction to Meal Prep
Meal prep is the process of planning, preparing, and portioning out your meals in advance. This can be done for a variety of reasons, including saving time, reducing food waste, and ensuring that you're getting the nutrients you need to support your fitness goals. When it comes to meal prep for fitness, it's all about finding a balance between protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats to fuel your workouts and support muscle growth and recovery.

## Benefits of Meal Prep for Fitness
Meal prep can have a significant impact on your fitness journey.
